What is CamTravel?

CamTravel is a travel platform that showcases Cambodia’s culture, nature, and tourist destinations. It helps visitors explore popular sites and plan their trips.

Our mission

Our mission is to promote tourism in Cambodia by providing useful information about attractions and experiences. We aim to make travel planning easier and more enjoyable. We also want to raise awareness about preserving culture and nature.

Who are we ?

We are second-year students at the Royal University of Phnom Penh, studying in the Department of Information Technology Engineering. We belong to Class A5, Generation 11. We are passionate about learning and applying IT skills.
